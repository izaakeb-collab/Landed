What this is

Enter a sealed box's price in Japan and LANDED works out your true cost to the door — exchange rate, proxy or export-shop fees, Japanese and international shipping, the import tariff, and customs — then tells you whether importing actually beats buying the same box in the US, and how many boxes you'd need to ship together to break even.
